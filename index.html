Top three things about Anambra State 😌

Attend a cultural festival 👹

The festivals are very exciting, with opportunities to see masquarades

Go to the Ogbunike Caves 🤯

Ogbunike caves are a natural wonder, also a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Never miss the food 🥰

There is a diverse array of mouthwatering local dishes that showcase the heart of Igbo cuisine.
